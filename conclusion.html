Conclusion

To reiterate, the usage of AI (artificial intelligence) has come to be of great magnitude in regards to the global reduction of academic integrity.

Primarily, this is due to:

  • Greatened Academic Pressure/Compulsion to Academic Proficiency - Students are prompted to use AI due to its effective and precise management of educational difficulties.
  • Increasing Prevalence/Accessibility of Artificial Tools - Consequent of the popularization that AI has undergone, many have begun to learn of it as well as tools that implement its functionality, resulting in their usage as swift remedies to academic dilemmas.

However, it is certainly feasible to address this problem.

With systematic, targeted efforts to:

  • Integrate collaborative academic work
  • Transition from monotonous, susceptible examination
  • Uproot academic dishonesty through synopses of its consequences

We can directly shape the actions of students and the future of academia on a global scale.
