The Impact of Artificial Intelligence:Destabilization ofAcademic Integrity

In the comprehension of the scientific development progressively occurring within our world, one might recourse to many different rationales regarding its furtherance, some of which include the contributions of brilliant minds, the conjoint efforts of international governments, and the staunch enthusiasm of the common public in the stimulation of global change; at the epicenter of these processes, though, in spite of their revolutionary nature, often lies a pure, genuine intent. The pertinence of this correlation to students, when seen through the lens of conscientiously completing educational work, is otherwise known as academic integrity. As such, this principle has continually served as a pillar bolstering the basis of ethics, veritability, and credibility in numerous fields of academia; however, with its constancy being increasingly subverted due to artificial intelligence (AI), how will we rise to protect it?
